Motivations/Reasons

My reasons for choosing this data set are as follows.

  1. My sister is always talking about how women are continentally miss represented in sports and how there aren’t enough facts to support why men are better at sports.
  2. I didn’t know I was going to end up with an Olympics data set, but I am happy I came across this, the Olympics are by far the most co-ed sport competition that I have ever come across. Women play against women and men against men, so it is a level playing field.
  3. I like this data set because the way it is broken up I am able to compare percentages and age groups based of gender very easily. It isn’t a matter of just saying men outnumber the women and there for are better. It becomes a matter of how many gold medals woman has compared to men based of the overall percentage of same gender competitors.
  4. The data set doesn’t require a lot of maintained. Many data sets that I came across when trying to fine sport date were over the top dramatic. They compared hotels and rest and how many trainers which is amazing but way over the top for what I needed this set for.
